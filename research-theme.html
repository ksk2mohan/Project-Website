Research Themes

AQVS focuses on interdisciplinary research in Quantum Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision and Smart Energy Systems. Through collaborative research and innovation, the project aims to develop intelligent surveillance technologies, predictive analytics and sustainable solutions for future smart energy infrastructures.
