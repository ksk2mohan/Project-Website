About the Visit

The AQVS project team visited Northumbria University, Newcastle upon Tyne, United Kingdom, to strengthen Indo–UK academic collaboration, explore advanced laboratories, exchange research ideas and promote joint educational initiatives.

Campus Tour

Exploration of academic facilities, research centres, innovation hubs and collaborative learning environments.

Research Laboratory Visit

Interaction with researchers working in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Robotics and Smart Energy Systems.

Academic Collaboration Meeting

Discussions on curriculum development, student mobility, joint publications and future research projects.

Visit Objectives

  • Strengthen Indo–UK academic partnership.
  • Exchange knowledge in Quantum Computing and AI.
  • Discuss future joint research proposals.
  • Promote faculty and student mobility.
  • Explore advanced research laboratories.
  • Develop innovative teaching resources.

Expected Outcomes

  • International collaborative research.
  • Joint workshops and expert lectures.
  • Student exchange opportunities.
  • Research publications.
  • Future British Council initiatives.
  • Industrial engagement with global partners.
