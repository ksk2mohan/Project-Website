About Project

Advancing Quantum Vision Systems (AQVS)

Real-Time Surveillance Skill Building for Smart Energy Facilities

AQVS is an Indo–UK Transnational Education initiative funded under the British Council Going Global Partnerships programme, bringing together academia and industry to develop expertise in Quantum Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision and Smart Energy Infrastructure.

  • Indo–UK collaboration involving VIT, Northumbria University and CEIR Mobility.
  • Capacity building through workshops, expert lectures and hackathons.
  • Research in Quantum Computing, Computer Vision, AI and Smart Energy Systems.
  • Development of future-ready talent and sustainable innovation ecosystems.
